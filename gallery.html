A Day At Chrysels

A Day At Chrysels

Our factory comes to life. Lights flick on.

Compressors start to roar. Computers awaken. Our pick-up vans roll onto the streets, transporting new images, new identities, and new signs ready to be installed. As the day progresses, projects are already in varying stages of fabrication throughout the plant.

Metal crafters, painters, electricians, and artists approach every project with the same level of enthusiasm, and pride in the quality of craftsmanship that defines each finished product.

The 40,000 square-foot facility

is underlined by progressive manufacturing systems, advanced tooling, and high-end materials. Each department interacts cohesively to create a compelling product unmatched in integrity and design. From concept to completion, our expert design engineering team acts as a liaison between all other departments – from permits to research, fabrication and installation.

At Chrysels Advertising LLC, the driving force is the latest technology we deploy. Our systems include digital illustration and design; high-pressure, cutting; CAD-driven machine routing; and large-format, full colour, digital display-making, to name a few. With all the technology at our fingertips, there is very little that we cannot do.
